Abstract

The best infographics transform complex information and data into graphics that are both easy to grasp and visually appealing.

Motivation

With infographics, you can create fresh content that communicates complex ideas to your audience at a glance.

From pie charts to pictograms, youâ€™ll have access to tons of different ways to represent your data.

General Design

We designed a tool which helps you to create infographics with data from .csv or .json file and design them in some forms that you choose in advance.

User Interaction

From the homepage, clients have the opportunity to choose from the features that our site expose from creating infographics. These are : charts and maps.

First of all, users have to choose what type of charts/maps they do want to display on their infographics by clicking the Insert button.

Some of the charts are just user friendly, used by the user for getting used with the concept of an infographics, so this way they could learn easily how an infographic works.
